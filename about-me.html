A little introduction: who am i?

Name: Ginny Cheung
Place of birth: Amsterdam I Date of birth: 01-09-1998

Hi! my name is Ginny. I'm currently studying at the HVA, majoring in CMD which stands for Communication and Multimedia Design. I've always been into designing. Designing allows me to express my creativity and bring it to life.

But another outlet that i have to express my creativity is through Photography. I've always loved taking pictures, hence the reason why i currently have 23.333 pictures stored on my phone. Most of them are just random pictures but thats not the point, the point is that i really like photography.

I've taken photography classes when i was in middle school and i've also considered as my bachelor course, which didn't passed. But i still want to keep my passion for photography alive and share my shots through this portfolio. Enough of my rambling, hope you enjoy it.
